Morning:

  • Arrive at Suvarnabhumi Airport or Don Mueang International Airport.
  • Transfer to your hotel in Bangkok and check in.

Afternoon:

  • Take a leisurely stroll around Khao San Road or visit the nearby Banglamphu area for some local sights and street food.

Evening:

  • Enjoy a casual dinner at a local restaurant or street food stall.
  • Explore the vibrant Patpong Night Market or take a relaxed walk along the Chao Phraya River.

Morning:

  • Head to Safari World, which is about an hour's drive from central Bangkok.
  • Explore both the Safari Park and Marine Park, enjoying wildlife shows, animal encounters, and various attractions.

Afternoon:

  • Have lunch within Safari World or at a nearby restaurant.
  • Return to your hotel to freshen up.

Evening:

  • Embark on a Chao Phraya Dinner Cruise. Enjoy a buffet dinner while cruising along the river, taking in illuminated views of Bangkok’s landmarks such as the Grand Palace and Wat Arun.

Morning:

  • Check out from your Bangkok hotel and take a bus or private transfer to Pattaya (approximately a 2-hour drive).

Afternoon:

  • Check in at your Pattaya hotel and have lunch.
  • Spend the afternoon relaxing on Pattaya Beach or exploring local markets and shops.

Evening:

  • Enjoy the Alcazar Cabaret Show, a famous transvestite cabaret with impressive performances, costumes, and sets.
  • Have dinner at a nearby restaurant or at the cabaret.

Morning:

  • Take a speedboat or ferry to Coral Island (Koh Larn). The trip usually takes about 30 minutes.
  • On the island, enjoy activities like snorkeling, parasailing, or simply relaxing on the beach.

Afternoon:

  • Have lunch on the island, and continue enjoying the beach and water sports.
  • Coral Island offers beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters.

Evening:

  • Return to Pattaya and relax.
  • Consider dining at one of Pattaya’s seafood restaurants or exploring the Walking Street for vibrant nightlife.

Morning:

  • Depending on your flight time, you might have a bit of time for last-minute shopping or relaxation in Pattaya.

Afternoon:

  • Check out from your hotel and transfer back to Bangkok or directly to the airport for your departure.
